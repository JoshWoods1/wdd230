Site Plan: Lampasas Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site name for this chamber of commerce website is "Lampasas Chamber of Commerce." We chose this name to reflect the city we serve and the purpose of promoting local businesses and community development.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to:

  • Act as a regional economic catalyst, fostering business growth and prosperity.
  • Provide a platform for members to connect, network, and access resources.

Scenarios

Some common scenarios for our website visitors include:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
